About Shreeja Biotech

Leading the Renewable Energy Revolution

Founded in Surat, Gujarat, Shreeja Biotech is a leading renewable energy company specializing in Waste-to-Energy (WTE), Bio-CNG (CBG) plant designing, and large-scale turnkey project execution. With a strong focus on cutting-edge biogas technology, anaerobic digestion, and carbon reduction strategies, we provide end-to-end solutions for industries, municipalities, and private enterprises looking to transition towards sustainable energy sources.

Our expertise in high-efficiency biogas plants, advanced feedstock preparation, and hybrid gas purification systems enables us to deliver innovative solutions that maximize energy recovery while reducing environmental impact.

Our Mission

"To develop, innovate, and implement world-class renewable energy solutions that transform organic waste into high-value, sustainable energy resources while promoting environmental responsibility."

Our Vision

"To establish Shreeja Bio-Tech as a global leader in Waste-to-Energy and Bio-CNG production, delivering cost-effective, sustainable, and high-performance solutions that contribute to a cleaner, greener future."

Our Bio-CNG Production Process

1

Waste Collection

Organic waste is sourced from agricultural, municipal, and industrial partners across Gujarat.

2

Pre-Treatment

Waste undergoes segregation, shredding, and preparation for optimal digestion.

3

Anaerobic Digestion

Microorganisms break down organic matter in oxygen-free digesters, producing biogas.

4

Biogas Purification

Our hybrid systems remove impurities to achieve 98% methane purity for Bio-CNG. Learn More

Feedstock Analysis

Comprehensive waste characterization to determine biogas potential and plant design, handling various feedstock variability.
